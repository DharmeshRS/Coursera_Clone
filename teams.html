What is Coursera for Teams?

Coursera for Teams is an online learning plan for a company or organization interested in purchasing for their team of 5-125 learners to master new skills. Coursera for Teams provides unlimited access to our catalog of 4,500+ courses, 400 Specializations, and more from top global universities and industries. We support multiple local currencies and offer a 14-Day Refund Guarantee.

navigate_next

What content is available for my team?

Coursera for Teams gives your learners unlimited access to over 4,500+ courses, Guided Projects, 400 Specializations, and Professional Certificates in over 100 subject areas and a variety of domains including data science, business, computer science, health, personal development, humanities, and more. It includes the majority of online courses on Coursera, taught by top instructors from leading universities and companies like Duke, Johns Hopkins University, Google Cloud, and more. Coursera for Teams does not include degrees or MasterTrack™ Certificates.

What is the learning experience?

Learners from your team will experience Coursera as any other learner on Coursera, through both web and mobile, with unlimited access to 4,500+ courses, Guided Projects, Specializations, and Professional Certificates in a variety of domains including data science, business, computer science, health, personal development, humanities, and more.

What is the difference between Coursera Plus and Coursera for Teams?

Coursera for Teams is the top solution for managing your team’s skill development objectives. With Coursera for Teams, you benefit from our suite of industry-leading program management tools. Track your team’s learning progress, manage invites and learners, and edit your curriculum, all in one place! Coursera Plus is a subscription for individual learners that provides unlimited access to the majority of the Coursera catalog and does not provide the program management tools required to upskill your team.

Can I get a refund for Coursera for Teams if it’s not right for my team?

We think you and your team will love Coursera for Teams, but to get a full refund, submit a refund request from your Account page within 14 days of your payment. Our team will process your refund right away with no questions asked. Any certificates earned within the 14 days will be revoked if you decide to refund within that period.

For additional information, see the refund policy on the Organization Help Center.

We accept payment through Credit Card and PayPal. For US-based companies, we also accept ACH Credit Transfer payable by invoice. Contact us to request an invoice or get started with Coursera for Teams today.
